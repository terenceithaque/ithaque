Platrithias is a village located 100 meters above the sea, 20 kilometers north of Vathy , and 3 kilometers from Stavros

The village is habited since the prehistoric times and is located in the Ithaca most fertile zone. Platrithias get 200 inhabitants, most of them working in farming.
