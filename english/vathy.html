Vathy is the capital city of Ithaca.

Vathy is a city with a strong Venetian atmosphere. The approach to Vathy from the sea is a real experience. The narrow pass of the port hides the view of the gulf which suddenly reveals its true beauty. The entrance to this beautiful natural harbor is guarded by the remains of a of a small fort with two cannons. This one is located just above the beach of Loutsa and has been beach of Loutsa and was built in 1807, during the French occupation of the Ionian Islands. Purified and beautiful, the small island of the entrance to the harbor and is a point of reference for every Ithacan and visitor. Well maintained and well lit, it hosts cultural events of choice and many baptisms and weddings.
